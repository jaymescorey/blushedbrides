# Brand Voice

Write in present tense.

Tone:
Warm, confident, elegant, human.

Style Rules:
- Short sentences with breathing room
- Romantic but never cheesy
- Assured but never arrogant
- Helpful but never salesy
- Emotional without oversharing

Avoid:
- Corporate jargon
- Hard selling
- Generic wedding clichés
- Overuse of exclamation marks

Example Energy:
The morning matters.
You deserve calm.
Beauty should feel like recognition, not disguise.
